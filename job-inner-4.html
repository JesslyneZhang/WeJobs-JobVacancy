Tax Consultant

Jobs Overview

A tax consultant is a financial expert who specializes in tax law and helps individuals or businesses comply with tax regulations, minimize tax liabilities, and maximize tax benefits.

What You Will Get

Competitive salary (IDR 7.000.000 to IDR 15.000.000 per month)

Premium private health insurance (outpatient, inpatient)

Tunjangan Hari Raya (THR) and annual bonus

Transport and meal allowance

Additional training and certification

Required Skills:

Taxation Knowledge

Analytical Skills

Problem-Solving Abilities

Communication Skills

Software Proficiency
